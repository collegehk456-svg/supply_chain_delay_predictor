# SmartShip AI: Antigravity-Level Hackathon Pitch

## Slide 1: Title Slide
**Title:** SmartShip AI - GenAI Logistics Intelligence Platform
**Subtitle:** Predicting delays, explaining risks, and optimizing supply chains with MLOps & Generative AI.
**Visual:** High-contrast dark mode dashboard snapshot showing real-time network anomaly monitoring.

## Slide 2: The Problem
- **Supply Chain Blindspots:** 60% of delayed shipments are only noticed *after* SLAs are breached.
- **"Black Box" Machine Learning:** Existing AI predicts delays but doesn't explain *why* or *what to do*.
- **The Cost of Delays:** High-value shipments stuck in remote warehouse blocks (D, E, F) cost the industry billions annually in expedited shipping costs and customer churn.

## Slide 3: The Antigravity Solution
**An end-to-end AI + MLOps logistics platform that:**
1. **Predicts** shipment delays with an XGBoost engine before they happen.
2. **Explains** risks using SHAP + Google Gemini Generative AI.
3. **Recommends** actionable business playbooks (e.g., "Upgrade to air freight for SKUs >3kg").
4. **Monitors** model drift and anomalies in real-time.

## Slide 4: Innovation & Tech Stack
- **AI/ML:** XGBoost, Scikit-learn, SHAP Values
- **GenAI Engine:** Google Gemini Pro for natural language insights
- **MLOps:** MLflow, Evidently AI, DVC, automated retrain pipelines
- **Backend/Frontend:** FastAPI, Streamlit Glassmorphism UI
- **Advanced Features:** Simulated Real-Time Weather Risk, Warehouse Congestion, AI Chatbot Assistant.

## Slide 5: The GenAI Explainability Engine
**From Numbers to Action:**
*Traditional ML Output:* "Delay Probability: 78%"
*SmartShip AI Output:* "This high-value shipment ($12k) is likely delayed. SHAP factors show high discount demand (25%) and heavy weight (>3kg) are driving the risk. **Recommendation:** Prioritize warehouse routing to Block A and upgrade to Flight transport to save $450 in SLA penalties."

## Slide 6: Business Impact & ROI
- **Reduces Delivery Failures:** Proactive alerts save 35% of at-risk SLAs.
- **Optimizes Operations:** Focuses human intervention strictly on high-risk, high-value packages using the AI Priority Engine.
- **Lower Logistics Costs:** Prevents over-discounting during peak shipping seasons.
